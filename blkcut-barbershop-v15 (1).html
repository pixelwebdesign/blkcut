FAQ

Got questions?

Everything you need before your first visit — or your hundredth.

Do I need to book in advance?+
We strongly recommend booking online — it takes 2 minutes and guarantees your spot with your chosen barber. Walk-ins are welcome when space allows, but we can't promise availability, especially on weekends.
How do I choose the right service?+
If you're unsure, book a Clean Slate (Classic Cut) and chat to your barber when you arrive. They'll advise on whether a fade, beard work or design would suit you — no pressure, no upsell.
Can I request a specific barber?+
Yes — you choose your barber as the first step when booking. You can see each barber's speciality and next available slot before selecting. Or choose "Any Available" for the first open slot.
What if I need to cancel or reschedule?+
We ask for 4 hours' notice so we can offer your slot to someone else. No penalties for genuine emergencies — just give us a call or reply to your confirmation email.
Does the barber get notified of my booking?+
Yes. When you confirm a booking, your chosen barber receives an automatic email notification with your name, service, and time. You'll also receive a confirmation email.
Do you do kids' cuts?+
Absolutely. Our Young Ones service is designed for under-12s and handled by barbers who are used to working with children. First cuts are always welcome.
What payment methods do you accept?+
We accept all major credit and debit cards, EFTPOS and cash. Contactless preferred but not required.
How long does a typical visit take?+
Service times are listed on the menu. A Classic Cut is around 35 minutes; The Works around 55. The BLKCUT Session takes 2 hours. We always finish properly — we won't rush you out.
Is parking available?+
Yes — there's a public car park directly behind 142 Devonport Road with 2 hours free. Street parking is also available on Devonport Road itself.
